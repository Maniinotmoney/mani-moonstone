How we can help you?

  • Basic English: Our Basic English course is designed for absolute beginners who are just starting their journey with the English language. Through a combination of interactive lessons, engaging activities, and practical exercises, students will build a strong foundation in grammar, vocabulary, pronunciation, and everyday communication skills. By the end of the course, students will be equipped with the confidence and ability to express themselves effectively in English.
  • Advanced English: For students looking to take their English proficiency to the next level, our Advanced English course offers a comprehensive curriculum that focuses on refining language skills and enhancing fluency. From complex grammar structures to advanced vocabulary and idiomatic expressions, students will delve deeper into the nuances of the English language. Through stimulating discussions, writing assignments, and presentations, students will develop the language proficiency needed to excel in academic, professional, and social settings.
  • IELTS/PTE Preparation: Are you planning to take the IELTS or PTE exam? Our specialized preparation course is designed to help you achieve your desired score. With expert guidance from experienced instructors, comprehensive study materials, and realistic practice tests, you will be well-prepared to tackle all sections of the exam. From mastering reading and listening comprehension to honing your writing and speaking skills, our course covers all aspects of the IELTS and PTE exams. Whether you're aiming for immigration purposes, academic admission, or career advancement, we'll help you reach your goals.

FAQ

Frequenty asked questions

What are the entry requirements for your courses?
  • There are no specific entry requirements for our Basic and Advanced English courses. Our IELTS/PTE Preparation course is suitable for students who have a minimum intermediate level of English proficiency.
How long are the courses?
  • The duration of each course varies depending on the program. Our Basic and Advanced English courses typically run for 3 Month, while our IELTS/PTE Preparation course is 6 Month.
Can I take classes online?
  • Yes, we offer both in-person and online classes to accommodate your preferences and schedule. Our online classes provide the same quality instruction and interaction as our in-person sessions.
What teaching methods do you use?
  • We employ a variety of teaching methods, including interactive lectures, group discussions, role-plays, multimedia presentations, and practical exercises to cater to different learning styles.
Do you offer any additional support for students?
  • Yes, we provide additional support services such as one-on-one tutoring, study groups, and access to online resources to help students succeed in their language learning journey.

At Moonstone English Center, we are committed to helping you achieve your language learning goals. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced learner, we have the courses and resources to support your learning journey. Join us today and take the first step towards mastering the English language!
