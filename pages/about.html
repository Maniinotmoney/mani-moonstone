Our 6-D Process

Define

We begin by defining the learning objectives and goals of our training programs. This involves understanding the specific needs and expectations of our students, as well as the requirements of the industry.

Design

Once we have defined our objectives, we design our courses to meet those goals. Our curriculum development team works closely with industry experts to create courses that cover all essential topics and skills in data science.

Develop

With the curriculum in place, we develop the necessary materials and resources to support our courses. This includes creating lecture materials, designing assignments and assessments, and developing interactive learning tools and platforms.

Deliver

The next step is to deliver our courses to our students. We employ a team of expert instructors who are passionate about teaching and dedicated to helping students succeed.

Deploy

As students progress through our courses, they have the opportunity to deploy their skills and knowledge in real-world projects and scenarios. We encourage students to work on practical assignments and collaborate with their peers to solve real- world data science challenges.

Drive

Finally, we drive our students towards success in their data science careers. We provide career support services, including resume assistance, interview preparation, and job placement assistance, to help students launch successful careers in data science.
